About the artist.

Abu Pashaev, born in 1966 in Chechen-Aul, is a distinguished artist whose life journey shapes his profound creations. From serving in the Soviet Army to graduating in graphic design, Pashaev's diverse experiences manifest in his artwork. Seeking guidance from renowned Chechen artists and integrating martial arts philosophy, he crafted his style, "Wind." His graphic series, like "You Don't Know How Painful It Is!," explores inner worlds with titles such as "Scream" and "Apocalypse."
